About us

DEF Serves society in the area of

Darshan Education Foundation (DEF) a non-profit, non-proprietary organisation was established in 1995 by Sant Rajinder Singh Ji Maharaj, Head, Science of Spirituality, which is a global non-profit organization based in Lisle, Illinois, focused on transforming lives through meditation. DEF family with its transcontinental identity is not merely a list of institutions, persons, or facts; it is a network of values, systems, and relationships. Spread across India & abroad, Darshan Education Foundation Society is committed to nurture and expand the family of 24 schools even in the remotest, most inaccessible part of the country as well as in multi-linguistic, multi-cultural setups outside the country. The foundation is committed to the creation of a value-based society by empowering individuals through holistic education and research having humanitarian values at its core.

Our Initiatives

Darshan Academy

DEF runs 24 Darshan Academy schools across India and Cali, Colombia. Over the last 25 years, DEF has provided students with a holistic, all- round education that focuses on their academic and moral progress.

Teach Peace Reach Peace

TPRP is a value based program on character and peace building to facilitate learning ethical values, compassion, conflict resolution, helping others, Selfless service.

Meditation and Research Group

MARG investigates the effects of practicing the SOS meditation technique on stress, anxiety and other well-being parameters using various scientific methods.

Meditation and Conscious Living Course

Students learn how to live a balanced and fuller life through focusing on living in present with the help of Meditation.

Padhenge to Badhenge

It is aimed at providing free-of-charge educational guidance and tutoring to underprivileged children, who are unable to afford proper education and coaching.

Language Classes

Darshan Education Foundation has undertaken various language teaching programs, latest of them being Urdu Classes named 'Ta'lim-e-Urdu' with the motivation of getting enlightened in the verses of Great Sufi Poets

Founder

Sant Rajinder Singh Ji Maharaj

Darshan Education Foundation was founded by our Visionary Founder Sant Rajinder Singh Ji Maharaj who is globally known as Great Saint, spiritual leader, a Scientist and a messenger of peace across the world. He preaches us to attain peace by connecting body, mind and soul through meditation. He had a dream to establish the kind of educational institutions, after the name of great sufi saint Sant Darshan Singh Ji Maharaj, which will provide not only quality and holistic education to children in every part of vast country but will also create generation of peace makers. He envisioned an Education that synthesizes Science with Spirituality. Spiritual Master tells us that when we become a fully integrated human being then our own full potential is realized. The inner energy is released. This brings about maximum benefits to ourselves and the world around us.
